Why this matters

A stronger man can strengthen the people around him.

Rise Up Kings describes its work as Christ-centered mentorship for men growing in faith, family, fitness, and finance. RSH Family Foundation supports that mission by making it easier for donors to understand the opportunity and contribute toward the work.

The goal is not to complicate the message. It is to make giving clear, personal, and easy.

About the foundation

What gifts can help make possible

  • Scholarship-style support for men who need help accessing growth programs.
  • Practical resources that point men toward faith, family leadership, health, and stewardship.
  • Awareness campaigns that help more people discover the work.
  • Recurring support that creates predictable monthly funding.
