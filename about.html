About the nonprofit

A focused way to turn personal conversations into practical support.

RSH Family Foundation gives Randy Harden a simple, trustworthy destination to share with people who want to learn about Rise Up Kings and contribute financially.

Mission

Support men who are ready to grow with purpose.

RSH Family Foundation raises funds and awareness for programs that help men strengthen their walk with God, lead their families with greater intention, care for their health, and steward their finances with wisdom.

This site keeps the message clear: learn the story, understand the impact, and give through a secure fundraising platform.

Built around the Rise Up Kings four-pillar focus

  • Faith: spiritual growth and Christ-centered direction.
  • Family: stronger leadership at home and in relationships.
  • Fitness: physical and mental resilience.
  • Finance: responsible stewardship and practical growth.
